I've since completed a Masters of Science in Management at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, where I deepened my understanding of leadership and organizational behaviour by connecting theory to my own real-world experience.

When I'm not at work, you can find me with my cat on my lap. I designed and built this website because I like learning new things.
